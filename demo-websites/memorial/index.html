Obituary

Eleanor Grace Smith, 84, of Springfield, passed away peacefully surrounded by her family on July 18, 2026. Born on March 14, 1942, to Thomas and Mary Johnson, Eleanor lived a life defined by compassion and grace.

She graduated from State University in 1964 with a degree in Education and dedicated over 30 years to teaching elementary school, touching the lives of countless children. In 1966, she married her beloved husband, Robert Smith, with whom she shared 58 wonderful years.

Eleanor was a devoted mother to her three children: David (Sarah), Emily (Michael), and James. She was the cherished grandmother to seven grandchildren who were the light of her life.

Beyond her family, Eleanor was an active member of the community garden club and volunteered faithfully at the local library. Her famous apple pie and her ability to listen without judgment will be deeply missed by all who had the privilege of knowing her.
