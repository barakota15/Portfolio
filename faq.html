Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Eventa Studio to create your perfect event website.

General

What types of events do you create websites for?
I create custom websites for virtually any event or celebration. This includes weddings, birthdays, corporate events, baby showers, graduations, engagements, festivals, memorial services, and family reunions. If you have an event to celebrate, I can build a site for it.
How long does it take to build an event website?
Depending on the complexity and package chosen, it typically takes 3 to 7 business days from the time I receive all your information and approval on the design concept. The Starter package can often be turned around in 3 days.
Do I need to provide the content?
You will need to provide the core details (dates, venues, photos, names, schedules), but I will handle the copywriting, formatting, and design layout to ensure it sounds and looks premium.
Can I see examples of your work?
Absolutely! You can view my portfolio page to see recent projects and explore live demos of different event types.

Pricing & Payment

What are your pricing packages?
I offer three main tiers: Starter ($499) for simple single-page events, Professional ($999) for weddings and medium events with RSVPs, and Premium ($1,999) for large-scale luxury events requiring custom features. View pricing details here.
Do you require a deposit?
Yes, a 50% deposit is required to secure your spot in my schedule and begin the discovery and design phase. The remaining 50% is due upon completion before handoff.
What's your refund policy?
I offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you are not completely satisfied with the initial design mockups before development begins, I will issue a full refund.

Technical

Will my website work on mobile devices?
Yes, every website I build is 100% responsive and tested thoroughly on smartphones, tablets, and desktops to ensure a flawless experience for all guests.
Do you provide hosting?
I provide guidance and recommendations for the best, most affordable hosting providers (such as Vercel or Netlify) and will handle the deployment process for you so you don't have to worry about the technical details.
Can I update the website myself?
Because these are custom-coded, pure HTML/CSS/JS sites designed for maximum performance and visual impact, they do not have a standard CMS backend. However, I provide clear instructions on how to make text and image swaps, or you can utilize my included support period.

Process & Support

How many revisions are included?
This depends on your package. Starter includes 2 revision rounds, Professional includes 4 rounds, and Premium includes unlimited revisions until you are perfectly happy.
What happens after the event is over?
Your website is yours to keep forever! Many clients choose to transform their event website into a post-event gallery to share photos and videos from the celebration.
